How to read the progress bar

The progress bar shows Shayan's score percentage after a test is submitted. Example: 16 out of 20 = 80%, so the bar fills to 80%.

Progress is saved in this browser on this device. If Shayan uses another device or clears browser data, the dashboard may not show previous attempts. Email and WhatsApp results still work separately.
