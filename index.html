How to read the progress bar

The progress bar shows Shayan's latest score percentage for that subject. Example: 16 out of 20 = 80%, so the bar fills to 80%.

Progress and history are saved in this browser on this device. If Shayan uses another device or clears browser data, this dashboard may not show previous attempts. Email and WhatsApp results still work separately.
